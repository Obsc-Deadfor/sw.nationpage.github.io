VC4-6M1

The VC3-6M1 Caracal(Ares: Karakaal) is a new generation infantry fighting vehicle made for the Arestikan armed forces.
Although developed in such a way to allow for service in any environment it serves best in environments such as Mars or the middle east following where there are significant Arestikan Territories.
It is intended to accomodate 8 infantry and 3 crew. It utilises standard Spaced Composite Armor Alloy with a plate of angled amorphous metal to the front of the IFV. It is much more heavily armed than its predecessor featuring two large ATGMs and a 49mm main gun.
On its roof it utilises a remote controlled 30mm for light AA purposes and a remote controlled 13mm for light targets.
It is theoretically resistant to all pulse weapons of a reasonable size allowing it to hold an edge in combat.
Specifications
Length: 8m
Width: 4.5m
Height: 4.2m
History
It was built as a successor to the VC3-5M series in ___ after the last war with the Imperium Martis led the Union to realise the shortcomings of the former. Because of its very recent production it has however only produced 1 variant being the VC4-6AM, an Amphibious assault capable vehicle. It also features both moving APS and non-moving APS.
