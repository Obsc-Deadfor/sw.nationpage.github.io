The Enceladus Equatorial Trench

The first megastructure built by the Arestikan Union it is a trench spanning over 1500km (the full diameter of Enceladus). Along the side of the structure lays vertical factories and refineries as well as smaller worker population centers. As such the bottom of the lays an industrial grade railway and various monorails dot the sides of the trench. On the surface surrounding the trench there are also various constructions, these being both defenses and shipyards. Due to its size it is visible from orbit
