Dragoon Class Destroyer

The Dragoon is a class of new age class of destroyers designed and used by the Ruimmarin of the Arestikan Union. Designed during the 3rd Fleet Modernization push to bring the smaller vessels up to fighting standard. It is a multi-use warship as it is able to both carry limited fighters and sub-craft. Its significant compliment of point-defense-cannons allow it to protect both itself and other naval assets from small craft and missiles/torpedoes. While its 380mm spinal railgun and missile launchers allow it to effectively take on ships above and matching its weight class. It also possesses 6 Quad mount 76mm coilguns manufactured by the KKW to allow it to lay down saturation fire and destroy smaller vessels effectively using Pansirveiergrenaat (PnsVrGrn TR-48) and Anpansirgrenaat ammunitions in all calibers of coilgun and railgun. It may also employ T-10 kill clouds to obscure sensors and destroy many types of munitions expected to be used against it. In terms of electronics it features long range phased array radars, and LIDAR, and may use its fleet uplink AI to share information gathered with the greater Ruimmarin. It is also a shielded vessel making it quite durable even without its layered armour package. These are all powered by its AG Hot Fusion Reactor. Due to its weaponry and goal it is expected to replace the older Halsey class cruisers. The Dragoon class is currently used by multiple Ruimmarin fleets for both in-fleet activity and cargo escort. a Large number of which are being built in and around Enceladus.

Specifications:
Length: 112m
4S engines, 2L engines, variable geometry
119 Units of storage.

Armament
x2 Anskip-450 150mm coilguns
x6 Quad Mount KKW 76mm coilguns
x6 Verd-449-55 49mm point-defense-cannons
x36 DLS Missile Launchers (72 missiles stored onboard)
x1 380mm Spinal Railgun

Propulsion and Power Generation
The destroyer is powered by a single GA Hot Fusion reactor which supplies power to both the weapons and its engines. It is propelled by 2 L-Standard Pattern variable geometry engines with an additional 4 variable engines for both assistance and maneuvering.
